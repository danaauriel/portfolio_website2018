He sat on a bench,

with his hair being a mess,

reading a blue book,

He watched the night sparkle,

as his blue book turned black.

He sat in silence,

with his book in his hand.

His big smile was gone.

It faded into the night,

and turned as black as the book.

A figure sat down,

humming a beautiful tune.

He looked up at her.

He saw her bright scarlet eyes,

sensed her trustworthy aura.

She smiled at him.

He stared into her bright eyes.

The darkness faded.

The light began to come back,

and she became all he knew.

They talked for hours.

It started with a hello.

She became his world.

His entire world consumed

by the girl with scarlet eyes.
