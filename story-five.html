Tanka 1

Lemony yellow,

A sparkly apple green,

Mesmerized in beauty,

How quiet each evening is,

So dull without the ablaze.

Tanka 2

Only birds chirping,

Sitting on a cold park bench,

Wind blowing the leaves,

Back and across my feet,

Down the brick path nobody crosses.

Tanka 3

Hope you recognize?

Or hope I get attention?

Somebody to see

Love I wish, Hope I wish for,

Dangling in front of me.
